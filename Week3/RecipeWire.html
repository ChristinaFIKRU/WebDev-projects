Kitfo (Ethiopian Spiced Steak Tartare)

[RECIPE IMAGE 800x400px]

Kitfo is an Ethiopian dish made with finely minced beef, seasoned with spiced butter and chili. Rich, bold, and deeply cultural; often enjoyed on special occasions or with loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb very fresh beef (sirloin or tenderloin), finely minced
  • 1/4 cup niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced clarified butter)
  • 1‚Äì2 tablespoons mitmita (Ethiopian chili spice)
  • ...

Instructions

  1. Ensure the beef is extremely fresh. Trim off any fat and finely mince it using a sharp knife.
  2. Gently warm the niter kibbeh over low heat until just melted.
  3. In a bowl, combine the minced beef, mitmita, salt, and optional spices.
  4. ...

Secret Tips

  • Kitfo can be served tere (raw), leb leb (slightly warmed), or yebesele (fully cooked)
  • Always source your meat from a trusted butcher if serving raw

About Me

Hi! My name is Chris and I am a photographer based in New York City. I specialize in travel and landscape photography, and I have a passion for capturing the beauty of the world around us. I have been taking photos for over 10 years, and I have had the opportunity to travel to some amazing places and capture some incredible moments. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new locations, trying out new photography techniques, and sharing my work with others. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
